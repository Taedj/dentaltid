## Tech Stack
- **Framework:** Flutter (Windows Desktop)
- **Backend:** Firebase (Firestore, Auth, Storage)
- **Database:** SQLite (Offline-First sync)
- **State:** Riverpod
- **UI:** Custom 3D Flip Cards & Framer-like animations
